The Limits

Chat AI has four
hard limits.

1

It can't see your files

You copy-paste code into a chat window, hoping you grabbed the right parts. It has zero idea what your project looks like.

2

It can't do anything

It gives you text. You copy that text, open another app, and apply the changes yourself. Every. Single. Time.

3

It forgets everything

Every conversation starts from zero. Last week's context? Gone. You repeat yourself endlessly.

4

It freestyles every answer

No structured process. No methodology. You get whatever it comes up with in the moment.

Chat AI is a brain in a jar.
Smart — but no hands, no eyes, no workspace.
